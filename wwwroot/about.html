About

Lech Wojas is a UX and UI Designer driven by fascination, interaction and technology. He spends most of his time creating intuitive user experiences working in an agile environment using a continuous process of research, design, prototyping and testing.

Lech currently works with Liberty Global in Amsterdam, working directly with Virgin, Ziggo and UPC brands. He previously worked with Channel 4 and Sonic Arts in London.
